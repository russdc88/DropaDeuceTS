Services

In the last 15 years of playing tennis, the biggest frustations I dealt with was how often I broke tennis strings and how much they cost to string. I want to solve that for others. We have over an assortment of strings that will fit your style for play, for half the price a club would charge. Racquet Consulting/Buying

When shopping for a racquet, our first option is to go to a club and ask. The problem with that is clubs are sponsored by specific tennis racquet brands, so they are going to try to sell you those racquets, in which they will raise the price to make money. I want to widen that scope at a more affordable price. I provide an in depth analysis on what racquet will fit you best based on a series of questions. I will then shop the market at both clubs and websites within your budget and find demo racquets for you to test.

Price: 8% commission of racquet price. Call or text me at (435)-862-2423 for additional questions.
